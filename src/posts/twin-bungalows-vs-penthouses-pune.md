When investing in the ₹3–10 Cr segment of the **Pune real estate market**, affluent buyers are usually presented with two primary options: an inner-city Penthouse, or a Twin Bungalow in a gated suburban estate. 

Here is why the market is aggressively pivoting toward the latter.

## 1. Land Ownership and Capital Appreciation
A penthouse offers spectacular views, but you do not own the land beneath it. In real estate, the structure depreciates while the land appreciates. **Twin Bungalows in Pune**, such as those in Supreme Villagio, come with a dedicated land parcel. This fundamentally guarantees higher long-term capital appreciation.

## 2. True Privacy
Even in ultra-luxury high-rises, you are still sharing a building. A Twin Bungalow offers absolute exclusivity. Your walls, your garden, and your driveway are yours alone.

## 3. The Wellness Factor
Post-2020, wellness has become the ultimate luxury. Penthouses are surrounded by urban smog and noise pollution. Properties in Somatane, nestled by the Sahyadri mountains, offer pristine air quality, natural light, and a resort-like lifestyle every single day.

For the modern HNI buyer, the Twin Bungalow is the undisputed champion of the **Pune luxury real estate market**.
