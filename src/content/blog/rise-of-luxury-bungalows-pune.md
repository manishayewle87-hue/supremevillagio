The landscape of Pune's luxury real estate is undergoing a massive paradigm shift. For decades, areas like Koregaon Park and Kalyani Nagar held the crown for premium living. However, as the city expands and the demand for space, nature, and exclusivity skyrockets, the epicenter of ultra-luxury living has decisively moved towards **West Pune**.

## Why West Pune?

The IT revolution in Hinjewadi and Baner has created a new class of homebuyers who demand more than just an apartment. They seek horizontal living—a return to the classic bungalow lifestyle, but with the modern amenities of a gated community.

### 1. The Need for Horizontal Living
After spending years in high-rise apartments, the modern affluent family is looking for ground-level living. The desire for a private garden, a personal parking space, and the sheer volume of a 4 or 5 BHK layout is driving the demand for [Twin Bungalows](/supreme-villagio/somatane/bungalows/twin-bungalows) and independent villas.

### 2. The Somatane Advantage
Situated perfectly between the bustling IT hubs and the serene hill stations of Lonavala, Somatane offers a micro-climate that is significantly cooler and less polluted than the city center. It is the perfect balance of connectivity and tranquility.

- **Proximity to Hinjewadi:** A seamless, traffic-free drive to the IT parks.
- **Nature's Embrace:** Surrounded by the Sahyadri mountains.
- **Future Infrastructure:** Proximity to the upcoming ring road and expressway developments.
