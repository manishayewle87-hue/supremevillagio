Hinjewadi is the beating heart of Pune's IT and tech sector, housing global giants and thousands of top-tier executives. For years, the default housing choice for these professionals was the luxury high-rise apartments located in Baner, Wakad, or within Hinjewadi itself. 

However, a massive shift in executive lifestyle preferences is underway. Today's tech leaders are actively moving away from vertical towers and are heavily investing in gated horizontal communities located just a short drive away.

Here is why locations like Somatane have become the premium choice for IT executives.

## 1. The Post-Pandemic Space Paradox
Working from home, or adopting a hybrid model, is the new normal for the tech sector. This shift exposed the limitations of apartment living. 

When you spend 60% of your week working from your residence, space becomes the ultimate luxury. Executives need dedicated, sound-proof home offices, private outdoor areas for breaks, and expansive rooms so family members aren't living on top of each other. 

A [Premium Row House](/supreme-villagio/pune/row-houses/premium-row-house) or a [Twin Bungalow](/supreme-villagio/somatane/bungalows/twin-bungalows) provides this massive volume of space that an apartment simply cannot match.

## 2. Escaping the Concrete Jungle
Hinjewadi and its immediate surroundings are densely populated and heavily concretized. After a high-stress day navigating complex tech projects, returning to a 20th-floor apartment surrounded by other towers offers little respite.

Just a 25-minute drive away, the environment changes drastically. Communities like [Supreme Villagio](/supreme-villagio/vision) in Somatane are nestled against the Sahyadri mountains. The air is cleaner, the temperature is cooler, and the surroundings are deeply green. It provides a psychological reset that is invaluable to high-performing professionals.

## 3. The Best of Both Worlds: Privacy + Amenities
In the past, moving to an independent bungalow meant sacrificing the security and amenities of an apartment complex. Gated horizontal communities have solved this problem.

At Supreme Villagio, you own your land and enjoy the ultimate privacy of a [Luxury Villa](/supreme-villagio/west-pune/villas/luxury-villas), without giving up five-star amenities. You have 24/7 multi-tier security, immaculate landscaping maintained by professional staff, and access to **Club Villagio**—featuring a state-of-the-art gym, swimming pools, and sports courts.

## 4. A Reverse-Traffic Commute
One of the best-kept secrets of living in Somatane while working in Hinjewadi is the commute. Because you are traveling against the major flow of city traffic on the Expressway and Highway, the drive is incredibly smooth and predictable. It is often faster to reach Hinjewadi from Somatane than it is to navigate through the dense city traffic from central Pune.

## Conclusion
For the modern IT executive, true luxury is no longer defined by how high up you live, but by how grounded you can be. Escaping the vertical density for a spacious, serene, and secure horizontal community is the ultimate lifestyle upgrade.
